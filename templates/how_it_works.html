How the Dementia Detector Works

Our dementia self-check tool is designed to help you understand your cognitive health in a simple, interactive way.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Start the Quiz
    Click the "Start the Check" button on the home page to begin your assessment.
  2. Step 2: Answer Cognitive Questions
    You’ll be asked 20 multiple-choice questions covering memory, attention, daily functioning, and problem-solving. These questions are designed by experts to screen for early signs of dementia.
  3. Step 3: AI Analysis
    Your answers are analyzed by an AI model trained to detect patterns associated with dementia risk. The model considers both correct answers and your daily functioning.
  4. Step 4: Personalized Results
    After completing the quiz, you’ll receive a personalized risk assessment, medical recommendations, and precautions to take.
  5. Step 5: Hospital Suggestions
    If your risk is moderate or high, you can select your city to get suggestions for nearby hospitals and specialists.

Why Early Detection Matters

  • Early diagnosis can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
  • Timely intervention allows for better planning and support.
  • Access to treatment and support services is easier when dementia is detected early.
